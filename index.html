Common questions

Do I need a neutral wire?

Usually no. Most Singapore homes don't have one at the switch, and our non-neutral switches work fine as long as your lights are 7W or above. A neutral wire is only needed for smart dimmer switches or the LCD panel switch. Read the full guide

Does the package include installation?

We set up the app and basic Google or Alexa voice control. Swapping the switches is done by your own electrician. LINE8 power track is installed by LINE8's team at $50 per track segment.

Can I use my own electrician for the LINE8 track?

You can, but LINE8 will not honour the warranty if their team didn't install it.

I'm getting one of your packages. How much to add a LINE8 track on top?

The LINE8 prices on this page are for self-contained bundles that include their own switches and hubs. If you're taking a Starter, Essential, or Premium package as well, text us and we'll quote the top-up for your exact setup so you don't pay for anything twice.

Does it work with Apple HomeKit?

Generally no. Our system is built for Google and Alexa. More on platforms

How is this different from Aqara?

Aqara is a closed ecosystem, so you buy everything Aqara. Ours runs on Tuya, so you can mix brands in one app. Open vs closed, explained

Does my WiFi need to be fast?

Coverage matters more than speed. Make sure you have enough mesh nodes or access points to clear the dead spots.

What if the WiFi goes down?

App control pauses until WiFi is back, but every smart switch still works by hand. Press it like a normal switch.

What lights do I need?

Only dimming needs smart lights. For everything else, normal lights are fine. Smart bulbs cost about 3 times a regular bulb, and they save you from wiring in a neutral-dependent dimmer switch. Smart bulbs vs dimmer switches

Do I need a smart aircon?

No. Keep your normal aircon and pair it with our smart remote to control temperature, fan speed, and mode from the same app. Smart aircons use their own separate app and won't integrate. Why we say skip it

My home is already renovated. Can I still go smart?

Yes. Smart switches are largely plug-and-play. Renovation only comes up in specific cases, like pulling a neutral wire for a panel switch or deepening a shallow switch box. The full story

Is there after-sales support?

Yes. We help you troubleshoot, and a technician visits if needed.

What warranty do you get?

Full first-year warranty on parts, labour, and service. Plus a free 2-year limited extension (parts covered) in exchange for a social media review.
